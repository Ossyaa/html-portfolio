The Best Movies According to Elizabeth

My top 3 movies of all-time

La La Land

A musical drama about the hardships of a relationship that falls apart due to individual life goals not lining up at the right time.

The Lion King

Someone out there thought to themselves, "Hamlet would be really cool if all of the characters were lions." and made it happen, and I think that is marvelous.

About Time

A romantic tragic comedy about a family whose sons can travel back in time by going into a closet. This movie tells the story of a son and his father, and the tragic consequences of knowing you can go in the past to visit your former self, and what changing the future can do to not only the individual, but the family as a whole.
